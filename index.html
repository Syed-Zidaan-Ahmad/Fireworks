Enjoy Zidaan's Fireworks !!!
For Phones: Shake/Move/Rotate/Tilt/Tap your phone in different directions or just simply move your finger on the screen.
For PC: Move the mouse cursor on the screen to see the magic

📲 How to Install it on your Device

For Phone:
1. Tap the ⋮ (three dots) on your browser
2. Tap “Add to Home screen”
3. Confirm to install

For Desktop:
1. Click the install icon in the address bar if visible (usually looks like a monitor with a down arrow)
OR
1. Click on the ⋮ (three dots) in the top right
2. Scroll down carefully
3. Click on (Cast/Save/Share) option and click “Install Fireworks”
